About Raleigh Home Automation Co.

Your trusted local experts for bespoke home automation solutions

Our Story

Raleigh Home Automation Co. was founded with a simple mission: to make smart home technology accessible, personalized, and truly useful for homeowners in the Raleigh area. We believe that every home is unique, and your automation system should reflect that.

Unlike one-size-fits-all solutions, we specialize in designing bespoke home automation systems that are tailored to your specific lifestyle, needs, and preferences. Whether you're looking to integrate voice assistants, set up security cameras, or build a comprehensive management system, we work with you every step of the way.

Why Choose Us?

Local Expertise

We're based in Raleigh and understand the unique needs of homeowners in our community. From local building codes to regional preferences, we've got you covered.

Bespoke Solutions

No cookie-cutter installations. Every system we design is customized to fit your home, lifestyle, and budget perfectly.

Expert Knowledge

We stay current with the latest smart home technologies and can recommend the best solutions for your specific situation.

Ongoing Support

Our relationship doesn't end after installation. We provide training, support, and are here to help as your needs evolve.

Our Approach

We start every project with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, lifestyle, and existing infrastructure. From there, we design a solution that integrates seamlessly with your home and daily routines.

Our services span the full spectrum of home automation:

  • Voice Assistant Integration: Seamlessly connect Alexa and Google Home with all your smart devices
  • Camera Systems: Expert recommendations and professional installation of security camera systems
  • Management Servers: Set up centralized control systems for advanced users
  • Custom Automation: Bespoke solutions for unique requirements

Serving Raleigh, North Carolina

As a local business, we're proud to serve the Raleigh community. The oak tree in our logo represents our connection to this great city, and we're committed to helping our neighbors create smarter, more efficient, and more secure homes.

Whether you're in downtown Raleigh, North Raleigh, or the surrounding Triangle area, we're here to help bring your smart home vision to life.
